This is a Ramen fanpage

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup featuring springy wheat noodles in a rich broth and topped with ingredients such as sliced pork, marinated egg, seaweed, and green onions, creating a beloved comfort food with deep umami flavors.

4 main ingredients

Broth:The flavorful liquid base, typically made from pork (tonkotsu), chicken (tori), or sometimes seafood/vegetables, forming the body of the soup.

Tare:A concentrated seasoning sauce (like soy sauce, miso, or salt) that gives the broth its primary salty, savory depth.

Noodles:Wheat noodles made with kansui, giving them their characteristic springiness and chew.

Toppings:Add-ins like chashu pork, soft-boiled eggs (ajitsuke tamago), nori (seaweed), bamboo shoots (menma), and scallions for texture and flavor.
